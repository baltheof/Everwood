ABOUT US

Escape to the Trees, Stay with Ease

Escape to EverWood, where cozy treehouses meet the tranquility of nature.
Nestled in a peaceful forest, our retreat offers the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

Whether you’re unwinding in the comfort of your treehouse or exploring the natural surroundings,
EverWood provides an experience designed for ultimate comfort and rejuvenation.

Enjoy the charm of the trees, the soothing sounds of nature, and the serenity of a secluded getaway,
all while having your comfort as our top priority. At EverWood, every moment is an opportunity
to relax, reconnect, and recharge.
